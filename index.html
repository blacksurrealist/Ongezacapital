What we do?

Ongeza Capital Actuaries & Consultants is a consulting company registered in 2018 to provide consulting services in the financial services sector.The company is domiciled in Johannesburg, South Africa but offers its services nationally.

The company is registered with the Actuarial Society of South Africa as an Actuarial Services Provider.
